# 🏡 Global Airbnb Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Airbnb listings across multiple global cities using **Power BI**.
It focuses on understanding **market trends, pricing strategies, customer behavior, and satisfaction metrics**.

The dashboard is designed to answer key business questions and provide insights useful for **hosts, investors, and platform analysts**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the growth of Airbnb over time
* Identify key pricing patterns across cities and room types
* Understand customer satisfaction and review trends
* Explore seasonal demand patterns
* Provide actionable insights for decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 🔹 1. Global Airbnb Market Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of Airbnb performance.

**Key Insights:**

* Airbnb listings and reviews peaked around **2019–2020**, followed by a decline due to COVID-19
* Cities like **Cape Town and Bangkok** have the highest average prices
* European cities dominate in listing density and engagement

**Questions Answered:**

* How has Airbnb grown over time?
* Which cities have the highest and lowest prices?
* Where are listings geographically concentrated?
* What is the overall market size and revenue potential?

---

### 🔹 2. Market Behavior & Customer Insights

This page focuses on deeper analysis of listings, pricing, and customer experience.

**Key Insights:**

* Entire homes command premium pricing compared to shared/private rooms
* Paris and New York have the highest listing volumes
* Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro lead in customer satisfaction
* Seasonal trends show peak demand during mid-year and holidays

**Questions Answered:**

* Which cities have the most listings?
* How does pricing vary by room type?
* What are the seasonal demand patterns?
* Which cities provide the best customer experience?
* How do Superhosts compare to regular hosts?

---

## 📈 Key Features

* Interactive filters (City, Room Type, Year)
* Dynamic visualizations (Map, Trends, Heatmaps)
* KPI indicators for quick insights
* Comparative analysis across cities and room types
* Seasonality analysis of reviews

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations and measures
* **CSV Data Sources** – Listings and Reviews datasets

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The analysis is based on two main datasets that is from Maven Analytics

### 🏠 Listings Data

* Price
* Room Type
* Availability
* Number of Reviews
* Host Information (Superhost)
* Location (City, Latitude, Longitude)

### 📝 Reviews Data

* Review Date
* Listing ID
* Customer Feedback

---

## 💡 Key Insights Summary

* Pricing is primarily influenced by **room type and location**
* High-demand cities maintain strong **customer satisfaction levels**
* Seasonal patterns significantly affect **review volume and engagement**
* Superhosts contribute to **higher quality and consistency**

---

## 🚀 Business Value

This dashboard can help:

* Hosts optimize pricing strategies
* Investors identify high-performing cities
* Platforms improve customer experience
* Analysts understand market trends
